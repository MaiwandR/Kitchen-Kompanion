Steak & mashed potatoes

Tonight's guided cooking flow for your household meal.

⏱️ 40 min 👥 serves 4 🔥 520 kcal/serving

Ingredients needed

Steak 4 cuts
Potatoes 6 medium
Butter 4 tbsp
Milk 1/2 cup
Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

1
Prep the ingredients

Wash and peel the potatoes, season the steaks, and set out the butter and milk.

2
Cook the potatoes

Boil the potatoes until soft, then drain and mash with butter, milk, salt, and pepper.

3
Cook the steak

Sear the steaks in a hot pan until they reach the preferred doneness for the household.

4
Plate and serve

Plate the steak with mashed potatoes and serve the meal for the family.
